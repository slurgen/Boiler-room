How i got interested in Information Technology

My interest in Information technology initially started with our first computer. When every 1 else were getting a commodore 64 we got an Atari 512 no hard disk just floppies. After outgrowing our first pc we got a used 386, from an add in the paper. Lucky the guy that sold it to use was very helpful and alerted us to the fact that computers we up gradable. From then on my brother and I would frequent auctions where old office computers were often sold on pallets of parts for cheap. We would buy bulk lots take them home and see what would fit, there were no instructions and no google. As we went on the opportunity to set up a network became available our first network was a ring daisy chain. Naturally our main motivator was to play games with each other quake, transport tycoon and I think scorched earth was in there too. Without going too far the rest was history my brother completed his degree straight out of year 12 where as I became obsessed with the opportunity to play games with others on a network.
